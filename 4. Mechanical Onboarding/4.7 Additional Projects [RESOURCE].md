# Additional projects
If you are interested in joining the Mechanical division but would like to do a project not listed above, undertaking another original project may be suitable, provided that you can use it to demonstrate learning and application of the skills Mechanical is looking for (see the readme).

Ideally, an onboarding project would be relevant to the needs of PAST or related to the projects PAST is currently undertaking, so that the skills you build during onboarding can be directly relevant to your PAST work (and ideally, allow your project to be manufactured, assembled, tested and delivered once you join PAST).

**Please contact a PAST member in the recruits discord if you're looking to do an unlisted project or would like help coming up with a project not on this list.**

## Ideal deliverables
- Demonstration of research into relevant field and identification of key ideas
- Demonstration of design thinking process via production of a rough design concept
- Demonstration of ability to use Inventor CAD tooling for assembly modelling, sketching and ideally basic FEA
- Demonstration of ability to create documentation about the process, concepts and actual design, in particular the ability to document an interface of a design for other components to mount to
- Demonstration of learning new concepts or skills throughout the process of the above deliverables
