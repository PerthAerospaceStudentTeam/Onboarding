# Team Health and Risk Monitoring 
This project involves building tools for leadership to monitor the health of the team. 

## Background
PAST relies on strong collaboration, sustainable workloads and clear leadership support to operate effectively as a team. As teams grow and project become more complex and heavy, it becomes hard to identify when members or leaders may be overloaded, disengaged or carrying responsibilities alone. This project focuses on building simple, practical monitoring tools that help PAST leadership maintain a healthy, sustainable and well supported environment. 

## Project Objectives 
-	Provide leadership with simple snapshot of overall team health 
-	Identify early signs of workload imbalance or disengagement 
-	Highlight leadership burnout or delegation risks 
-	Support long-term sustainability and knowledge sharing 

## Project Deliverables 
1.	Team Health One Page Report 
-	Project planned vs. completed 
-	Attendance or participation trends 
-	Time recruited to first meaningful contribution 
-	Skill vs. workload balance 
2.	Leadership load risk matrix 
-	Leadership responsibility mapping 
-	Burnout and delegation risk indicators 
-	Single points of failure identification 
-	Risk rating system 
-	Suggested mitigation strategies
