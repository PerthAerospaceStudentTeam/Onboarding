# Member Wellbeing – Prevention, Intervention and Life Balance Framework 
This project involves creation of a structured framework for supporting member wellbeing and team performance in a sustainable manner.

## Background
Balancing university, technical projects and personal commitments can lead to burnout and disengagement. A structured sustainability framework supports member wellbeing while maintaining team performance. 

## Project Objectives 
-	Identify early signs of disengagement or overload 
-	Develop support and escalation pathways 
-	Create realistic participation expectation frameworks 
-	Develop lightweight team health monitoring tools 
-	Identify leadership burnout and succession risks 

## Project Deliverables
1.	Member workload and expectation guide 
2.	Burnout and disengagement indicator checklist 
3.	Support and escalation pathway flowchart 
4.	Team health one-page monitoring template 
5.	Leadership and succession risk matrix
