# New member 60 Day Implementation 
This project involves mapping the journey of new recruits over their first 60 days of onboarding and becoming new members, to improve experiences and outcomes.

## Background
The first 60 days of joining PAST are critical for member confidence, engagement and long-term retention. New recruits often experience uncertainty about expectation, responsibilities, support systems and how to contribute.  This project focuses on designing a structured 60-day onboarding journey that supports new members from initial induction through to confident team contribution. The framework should provide clear milestones, expectation and support systems. 

## Project Objectives 
-	Create structured onboarding pathway covering the first 60 fays of membership 
-	Support clarity of expectations and responsibilities 
-	Support early engagement and meaningful contribution 
-	Provide clear support and escalation pathways 
-	Strengthen retention and member confidence

## Project Deliverables 
1.	Create clear timeline outlining key onboarding stages and milestones
      - Orientation and familiarising 
      - Integration and skill development 
      - Contribution and confidence building 
2.	Checklist that helps track onboarding progress 
3.	Support and escalation pathway guide 
      - Mentor or buddy support systems 
      - Team leadership support pathways 
      - Technical project or project support 
4.	Quick start onboarding guide
