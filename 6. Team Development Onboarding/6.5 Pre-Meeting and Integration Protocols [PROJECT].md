# Pre-Meeting and Integration Protocols 
This project involves creating tools to assist with consistent meeting prepration and meeting outcomes.

## Background
Meetings are a core part of collaboration, decision making, and knowledge sharing within PAST. However, inconsistent meeting preparation, unclear expectations and limited follow-up can reduce productivity and make it harder for new members to engage confidently. This project will focus on creating simple and consistent meeting preparation and integration protocols. The goal is to improve meeting efficiency, increase member participation and support smoother onboarding into team workflows. 

## Project Objectives
-	Standardise how meeting are prepared, run and followed up (templates, end of meeting notes everyone can access) 
-	Improve clarity of expectation for meeting participants (weekly vs monthly vs standups) 
-	Support new member integration into meeting and team discussions 
-	Reduce confusion, repetition and communication breakdowns 
-	Create sustainable meeting practices that can be used across teams 

## ☑️ Project Deliverables
1.	Pre-Meeting Preparation Tool 
-	Meeting purpose and outcome 
-	Agenda development template 
-	Participation expectations 
-	Role clarification 
2.	Meeting Integration Guide for New Members 
-	Meeting structure overview 
-	Participation expectations 
-	Post meeting follow-up expectations 
-	Support pathways
