# Feedback and Growth Development Loop 
This project involves developing a feedback and growth loop model to assist leads with understanding how and why to deliver effective, consistent and constructive feedback. 

## Background
Consistent, constructive feedback is essential for leadership development and member growth. Student teams rely on informal feedback, which can limit development and reduce psychological safety. 

## Project Objectives
-	Design structured feedback cycles 
-	Support leadership skill development 
-	Link feedback to personal growth planning 
-	Follow-up tracking structure 

## Project Deliverables
1.	Feedback cycle timeline 
2.	Feedback conversation templates 
3.	Growth and development planning guide 
4.	Follow-up tracking structure
