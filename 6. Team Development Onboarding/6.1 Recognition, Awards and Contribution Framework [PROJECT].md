# Recognition, Awards and Contribution Framework
This project involves creating a framework for recognition, awards and contributions of PAST members.

## Background
Recognition within student engineering and software teams usually focuses on major achievements, which can overlook consistent effort, teamwork and growth. Implementing a structured recognition system will help to improve individual and team motivation, retention and team culture by celebrating both large and small contribution. 

## Project Objectives
-	Design a multi-layered recognition award system (going beyond ‘member of the month’) 
-	Create transparent and fair award categories 
-	Align with PAST values and behaviours 
-	Support peer-to-peer appreciation culture
-	Clear strategies to implement growth in the team towards achievement 

## Project Deliverables
1.	Recognition system and structure
2.	Award categories and contribution criteria 
3.	Peer nomination and recognition process and format (what the process will look like). 
4.	Recognition guidelines for leadership use 
5.	Optional badge or call-out examples (e.g. discord channel for recognition)
