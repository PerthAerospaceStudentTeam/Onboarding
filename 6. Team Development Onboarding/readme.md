# Team Development Onboarding

The Team Development department is responsible for supporting the learning and skill development of PAST members, sponsorship and business planning, human resources and conflict management, recruitment and onboarding, social events and outreach. The work of Team Development is crucial to growing and maintaining a happy and productive team environment.

If you would like to become a Team Development member, you must have evidence of Team Development work in your logbook. If you develop an interest in Team Development after onboarding, then please speak to a Team Development member, we would to have you on the team!

NOTE: If you have ideas for a project not specified on this GitHub, please talk to a Team Development member (@Liria(Team Dev) or @Annabelle(Team Dev) on Discord).
