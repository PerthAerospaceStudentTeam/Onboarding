# Team Systems and Integration Mapping 
This project involves the creation of mapping and planning tools to clearly identify decision making channels, organisational structure and expectations. 

## Background
As PAST grows, new members as well as current members can experience confusion around decision pathways, meeting structures and role expectation. Mapping team systems and creating integration protocols supports clarity, efficiency and smoother onboarding. 

## Project Objectives
-	Map team structures and decision-making flows 
-	Identify communication bottlenecks 
-	Standardise meeting preparation and follow-up 
-	Improve integration for new members 

## Project Deliverables
1.	Team system and decision flow map 
2.	Meeting preparation and facilitation protocols 
3.	New member meeting integration guide
4.	Identified friction point report (where new and current members struggle to integrate)
