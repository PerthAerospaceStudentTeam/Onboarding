## Suggested Workflow

### 1. Review and Analysis
- Review PAST‚Äôs current Instagram profile
- Identify what types of posts perform well
- Note areas for improvement (e.g. frequency, format, storytelling)

### 2. Research and Inspiration
- Explore social media accounts from:
  - University engineering and aerospace teams
  - Student societies and project teams
  - CubeSat and aerospace companies
- Save examples of posts, reels, and storytelling styles you like

### 3. Ideation
- Brainstorm content ideas such as:
  - Team introductions and ‚Äúday in the life‚Äù clips
  - Project progress updates
  - Reels explaining subsystems or concepts at a high level
  - Launch throwbacks or build highlights
  - Recruitment-focused content
- Prioritise ideas that are simple to film and repeatable

### 4. Planning
- Create a simple content calendar
- Decide:
  - What will be posted
  - When it will be filmed
  - Who needs to be involved
  - What equipment is required
- Consider aligning posts with milestones, events, or launches

### 5. Content Creation
- Film and edit content following branding guidelines
- Add captions, hashtags, and on-screen text where appropriate
- Ensure content is accessible and easy to understand

### 6. Feedback and Iteration
- Share drafts with recruits and senior members
- Collect feedback on clarity, tone, and branding
- Make revisions as needed
